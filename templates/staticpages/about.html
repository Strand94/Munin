About Munin

Munin is a project developed by 4 masters students at NTNU for TDT4140 - Programvareutvikling.

The purpose of the project is revolutionizing the teaching experience at universities around the world,

And the man responsible for all of this is this man: Pekka Abrahamsson

To achieve this, we decided to create a webpage, the likes of which the world has never seen

Munin helps students ask questions and get answers from lecturers

Students enroll in a class and are connected to a subject

Once there, they may ask questions, and upvote previously asked questions, to let the lecturer know

what the students want explained. They can also search through already answered questions to get answers.
