About Munin

Munin is a web browser based learning enhancement tool for universities and learning institutions, based upon making
feedback between lecturer and student easier and more efficient.

Munin:
  • Pools together questions in classes, and makes the most popular questions the most prominent.
  • Saves answered questions, letting student search through a plethora of answers between classes.
  • Let the students themselves have a crack at answering questions, with the teacher letting the best answer stand.
